Best horror movies or series of all time according to Arpan Simon

My top 3 horror movies -

Hereditiary

A truly bone chilling horror movie which is very different from usual horror movies. It doesn't rely on a lot of jump scares, rather focuses is on the narrative.
Caution : This one's not for the faint hearted

Haunting of Hill House

A masterpiece created by Netflix with a captivating story line and amazing characters.
P.S. I've never seen a horror movie / series with such a compelling (and emitional) story.

Grave Encounters

One of the older movie - portraying horror from the first person view - similar to blair witch project.
This one was the reason for a lot of my childhood nightmares :)
